Nutritional Evaluations

View which brands appear most frequently in the database, or which nutriscore and ecoscores appear most frequently. Click the dropdown menu to select a category to view. The size of the bubbles corresponds to the number of products in that category. Click on a bubble to view the distribution of brands in that scoring category, or the distribution of nutriscore and ecoscores for a specific brand. Click the "Back" button to return to the main view.

The data is sourced from the Open Food Facts database, which contains information on over 1.5 million food products from around the world. The database includes information on ingredients, nutritional content, and various scores such as Nutri-Score and Eco-Score.

Score Explanation

The Nutri-Score is a nutritional rating system that assigns a letter grade (A to E) to food products based on their nutritional content, with A being the healthiest and E being the least healthy.

The Eco-Score is an environmental rating system that assigns a letter grade (A to E, and sometimes A+) to food products based on their environmental impact, with A being the most environmentally friendly and E being the least environmentally friendly.
